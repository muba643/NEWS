Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "News ፈጣን መረጃ" service ("the Service") handles information.

1. Data We Collect

The Service does not collect, store, or process any personal data from the people who read or follow it on Telegram, the mobile app, Facebook, or TikTok. There are no user accounts, sign-ups, or tracking of individual readers.

2. What the Service Processes

The Service reads publicly available RSS news feeds and processes headline/article text through Google's Gemini AI API to generate summaries and translations. No personal or user-submitted data is sent to this API — only publicly published news article text.

3. Third-Party Platforms

Content is distributed via Telegram, and optionally Facebook and TikTok. Each of those platforms has its own privacy policy governing how they handle any data related to your use of their respective apps, which is outside the control of this Service.

4. Mobile App

The companion mobile app fetches a public news feed file and stores a single local preference (your chosen display language) directly on your device. This preference is never transmitted anywhere and cannot be accessed by the Service operator.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, since no data collection takes place at all.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the Telegram channel: @news_fetan_mereja.
